## Objective

The SIG aims to design and deliver robust, optionally enforceable hierarchical namespaces
as first-class citizens in Rucio. We will strengthen and formalise hierarchy enforcement,
eliminate inconsistencies around the use of / in DIDs by overhauling affected APIs,
policies, and workflows. Our objective is to make hierarchical namespaces easy to adopt
without disrupting existing deployments.

## Expected End-Date

April 2027

## Means to achieve the objective

- Mattermost channel: [#hierarchical-namespaces](https://mattermost.web.cern.ch/rucio/channels/hierarchical-namespaces)
- eMail list: rucio-sig-hierarchical-namespaces@cern.ch
- Events and ad-hoc meetings with interested communities either over Zoom or asynchronously
- Community shepherds
  - The convener is assisted by a team of community shepherds to ensure that the changes
    address the needs of communities requiring hierarchical namespaces, but as well to
